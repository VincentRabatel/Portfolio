About me

After studying as a 2D/3D graphic designer at Rubika Supinfogame in Valenciennes, France, I now occupy a pivotal position between all the specialized teams for the creation of interactive digital content.

By focusing on the engine side, I'm able to take care of a whole part of my teammates' workflow (visual artists, game & level designers, sound designers, narrative designers...), to allow them to fully express their respective talents.

For your projects I can take care of :

  • 👉 prior coordination on how to create the assets
  • 👉 integration of all assets into the engine
  • 👉 construction of scenes and levels, ensuring artistic direction and design specifications
  • 👉 optimization and final polish with a view the project's release


I also adapt to several types of projects :
  • 💻 Video games
  • 💻 Interactive experiences
  • 💻 Mobile apps
  • 💻 AR / VR
